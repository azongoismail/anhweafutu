About Us

Anweafutu Basic School is a public basic school located in the Western North Region of Ghana, specifically in the Juaboso District. It is a government-funded educational institution that provides Basic education to children between the ages of 6 and 20.

The school was established to provide quality education to children in the Anweafutu community and its surrounding villages. The school is situated in a rural area and serves a diverse student population from various ethnic and socio-economic backgrounds.

The curriculum of Anweafutu Basic School is designed to provide a strong foundation for future learning. It includes core subjects such as English, mathematics, social studies, and science, as well as Ghanaian cultural studies. The school also offers extracurricular activities such as sports, music, and drama to encourage holistic development in its students.

The school has a dedicated teaching staff that is committed to providing high-quality education to its students. The teachers at Anweafutu Basic School are well-trained and experienced in their respective subjects, and they use modern teaching methods to enhance the learning experience of the students.

In addition to the core academic curriculum, Anweafutu Basic School also provides various support services to its students. This includes guidance and counseling services for students who may need extra support.

Despite its rural location, Anweafutu Basic School has made significant strides in providing quality education to its students. The school has a good track record of academic achievement, with many of its students performing well on national exams.

In summary, Anweafutu Basic School is a vital educational institution that provides a strong foundation for future learning to children in the Western Region of Ghana. It is committed to providing quality education and support services to its students, and its teachers are dedicated to ensuring the success of its students.
